What's Currency?

Currency is a medium of exchange used in trade and commerce. It represents value and facilitates transactions.

Differnt countries have different currencies which varry in value from one country depending on the country and other factors.

  1. US dollar - United States
  2. Euros - European Union
  3. Sterling Pound - United Kingdom
  4. Chinese Yuan - China
  5. Japanese Yen - Japan

Currently, the US dollar has been the major currency adopted by most countries for their international trade with other countries
